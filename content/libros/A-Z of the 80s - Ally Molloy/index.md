Take a step back in time to the 1980s! Return to the glamorous decade that brought us Footloose and The Breakfast Club, where leg warmers and shoulder pads were all the rage, and when nightclubs blasted classic tunes by Spandau Ballet, Wham and Madonna.

With hundreds of enteries from 'A is for... The A-Team' to 'Z is for... ZZ Top', be prepared to relive the punk and the glam and everything in between.

The A-Z of the 80s is your complete guide to the most extravagant and fun decade of the twentienth century.
